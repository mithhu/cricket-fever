# Cricket Fever — Roadmap & Planning

> Vision: Build a fun, polished 3D cricket game in the browser, inspired by EA Cricket 07, using Three.js and vanilla web technologies.

---

## Current State (v0.1 — MVP + Post-MVP)

- Full batting experience against AI bowling
- 4 shot types with distinct full-body animations and footwork
- 9 fielders with chase, dive, and physical catch mechanics
- Player profiles with persistent high scores
- Pause/resume functionality
- Broadcast camera with ball tracking

---

## Phase 1: Polish & Game Feel (Next)

**Goal:** Tighter gameplay, better visual feedback, more satisfying feel.

- [ ] Sound effects — bat crack, crowd roar, wicket fall, boundary cheer
- [ ] Commentary snippets — text-based event commentary
- [ ] Ball trail/glow effect on fast deliveries
- [ ] Replay camera — slow-mo replay of boundaries and wickets
- [ ] Better stumps animation — flying bails on bowled
- [ ] Crowd animation — simple crowd movement in stands
- [ ] Improved outfield — grass texture variation
- [ ] Difficulty levels — easy, medium, hard (bowling speed and accuracy)

---

## Phase 2: Bowling Mode

**Goal:** Let the player bowl and have AI bat.

- [ ] Bowling controls — line, length, speed selection
- [ ] AI batsman — shot selection based on delivery
- [ ] Bowling animations — fast, medium, spin variations
- [ ] Bowling stats — economy, wickets, overs

---

## Phase 3: Full Match

**Goal:** Two-innings match with target chasing.

- [ ] Two-innings gameplay — bat first, then chase
- [ ] Target display and required run rate
- [ ] Match result — win/loss/tie
- [ ] Toss — coin toss to decide bat/bowl

---

## Phase 4: Teams & Customization

**Goal:** Team selection and player customization.

- [ ] Team selection — multiple teams with different kits
- [ ] Player customization — batting style, jersey number
- [ ] Tournament mode — series of matches
- [ ] T20 and ODI formats

---

## Phase 5: Multiplayer

**Goal:** Play against friends online.

- [ ] WebSocket-based multiplayer
- [ ] Lobby system
- [ ] Real-time match synchronization

---

## Priority Order

1. Sound effects and game feel polish
2. Difficulty levels
3. Bowling mode
4. Full match (2 innings)
5. Teams and customization
6. Multiplayer
